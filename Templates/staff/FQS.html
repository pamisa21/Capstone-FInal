Frequently Asked Questions

Faculty performance is evaluated based on student feedback categorized into three sentiment categories: Positive, Negative, and Neutral. These categories help assess teaching effectiveness and areas for improvement.

The sentiment categories are as follows:


Positive: Positive feedback indicating satisfaction with the faculty member's performance.

Negative: Negative feedback indicating dissatisfaction with the faculty member's performance.

Neutral: Neutral feedback that doesn't strongly favor either positive or negative.

You can filter faculty evaluations based on sentiment using the provided filters in the dashboard. This allows you to view feedback categorized by Positive, Negative, or Neutral sentiments, giving a clearer overview of faculty performance.

The overall sentiment for each faculty is determined based on the combination of positive, negative, and neutral feedback received. This is visualized on the dashboard using a sentiment breakdown chart and a pie chart to help assess overall performance.
